About The Academy

Where Dedication Meets Direction

Prashasti IAS Academy, Mohanpuri Meerut mein sthapit, ek premier coaching institute hai jo UPSC Civil Services Examination aur UP-PCS ki taiyari kar rahe students ko top-notch guidance pradan karta hai. Hamara mission hai aspirants ko sahi disha, structured study material aur expert mentorship dena — jisse wo apna IAS/IPS/PCS banne ka sapna sach kar sakein.
